This is Bob.

Bob is a surveillance agent sent out by an undisclosed advertisement company in the mid 2600s
Every conscious being from the beginning of time, who knows their name as bob, has a portion of their brain controlled and inspected by Bob for their whole life
He sits inside their brain and looks out, taking notes which he will relay to the company at the end of his mission.
In order to insure he ends his mission, he inhabits the bobs from the shortest living bobs to the longest living bobs:
with something such as a tragedy at the start, and his own eternal afterlife at the end.

Because of this, we know that he does finish his task, as he has been seen roaming the sites of death
Therefore, we can sadly deduct that no being named bob will live forever
Unless he does one day die in here,
which is, to our understanding, impossible
